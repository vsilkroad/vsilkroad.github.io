About us.

At VideoSilkRoad, we are a venture-backed Beijing-based start-up focused on video content & technology.

Our mission is to help international video content owners to monetize from China market, particularly those who do not have a China operation already.

Our strength is that we are familiar with the Chinese online video ecosystem . For example, Youtube is not accessible in China; the distribution is complicated as there are many Chinese Youtube clones , each of them is sizable but none achieve monopoly; language and regulation barrier, etc.

Essentially, we are proposing to be your video content distribution partner in China, and we could help you to promote brand recognition and achieve additional revenue out of this previously untapped market.
